Dinuguan Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pork 700g
  • Pork Blood, 2 cups
  • Bay leaves, 2 pcs
  • Vinegar, 1/4 cup
  • Garlic, 4 cloves
  • Onion 1 whole
  • Ginger, thumb-sized
  • Green Chili pepper (siling pinagang), 4pcs
  • Sugar, 2 tbsp or to taste
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • water, 2 cups

How to Cook

  1. Heat oil and fry the onion, garlic and ginger for a few minutes until they turn light brown.
  2. Add the pork and saut√© for 15 minutes until the color changes.
  3. Add the vinegar, bay leaves and water. Don't stir it first. Cover the pan and let it simmer for half an hour.
  4. Next put the pig's blood. Lower the flame. Mix slowly and continuously until the sauce thickens and to avoid lumps.
  5. When the soup is thick, add the desired length. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Just mix it and let it cook for 5 minutes before switching off the flame.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve it with puto or hot rice.
